Frequently asked questions

Q: What types of events do you cater for?
A: We cater for a wide range of events, including corporate events, private parties, birthdays, and more. No event is too big or too small for us to handle.

Q: Can you accommodate dietary restrictions or special requests?
A: Yes, we are happy to accommodate dietary restrictions, allergies, and special requests. Just let us know in advance, and our chefs will work with you to create a customized menu that meets your needs.

Q: How far in advance should I book your catering services?
A: We recommend booking our catering services as early as possible to ensure availability for your desired date. However, we understand that sometimes events come up last minute, so feel free to reach out to us, and we will do our best to accommodate your request.

Q: Do you provide staff for events?
A: Yes, we can provide professional staff, including servers, bartenders, and event coordinators, to ensure that your event runs smoothly. Just let us know your staffing needs, and we will take care of the rest.

Q: What is your cancellation policy?
A: Our cancellation policy varies depending on the circumstances of the event. Please reach out to us directly to discuss any cancellations or changes to your catering order.
